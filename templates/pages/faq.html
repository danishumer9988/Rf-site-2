01 What is Job Reference Hub? ▼

Job Reference Hub is a career platform that connects job seekers with employers. We list thousands of job and internship opportunities across various industries, with a focus on tech, remote, and on‑site roles.

02 Is Job Reference Hub free? ▼

Yes, creating an account, browsing jobs, and applying for positions is completely free for job seekers. Employers may pay for premium job postings or featured listings.

03 How do I find jobs and internships? ▼

You can use the search bar on the homepage to filter by keyword, location, job type (remote/on‑site), or category. You can also browse by industry or use the "All Jobs" and "All Internships" pages.

04 How do I save a job or internship? ▼

When you are logged in, click the heart icon (🤍) on any job or internship card. The icon will turn red (❤️) and the listing will be saved to your "Saved Jobs" or "Saved Internships" section in your dashboard.

05 How do I apply for a job? ▼

Click the "View Details" button on a job card, then follow the application instructions provided by the employer. Some listings may allow you to apply directly through our "Quick Apply" modal.

06 How do I create an account? ▼

Click the "Sign Up Free" button in the top‑right corner of the page. Fill in your email, choose a password, and complete your profile to start applying.

07 I forgot my password. What should I do? ▼

On the login page, click "Forgot Password?" and follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

08 How do I update my profile? ▼

Log in and go to your Dashboard, then click "My Profile" or "Settings" to update your personal information, resume, and preferences.

09 How do I post a job or internship? ▼

After logging in as an employer, click the "Post a Job" link in the navigation bar. Fill in the required details (title, description, location, etc.) and submit. Your listing will be reviewed and published.

10 Can I edit or remove a job posting? ▼

Yes, go to your Dashboard and select "My Job Posts." From there, you can edit or delete any of your active listings.

11 Is my data secure? ▼

We take data security seriously. We use encryption, secure servers, and regular monitoring to protect your personal information. For more details, please read our Privacy Policy.
