Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information.

01 Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as when you create an account, apply for a job, post a job, or contact us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, resume, and any other details you choose to share.

We also automatically collect certain data when you visit our site, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and referring URLs.

02 How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services — including job matching and recommendations.
  • To communicate with you — about your account, applications, or job postings.
  • To send you job alerts — newsletters and promotional content (you may opt out at any time).
  • To analyze site usage — and enhance user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations — and prevent fraud.

03 Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings.

04 Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your data with:

  • Employers or recruiters — when you apply for a job or internship.
  • Service providers — who help us operate our platform (e.g., hosting, analytics, email delivery).
  • Law enforcement — or regulatory bodies when required by law.

05 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

06 Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at:

📧 privacy@jobreferencehub.com

07 Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

08 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our site or sending an email.
